Morning drive to Corbett National Park, Drive past to "Garhmukteshwar" one of the famous pilgrimage place for Hindus. Pilgrims from nearby places come here to take a bath in Holy Ganges. On arrival at Corbett, check in at wildlife resort. Lunch will be served at wildlife Resort. After lunch we will take you to "Corbett Fall" a scenic place near Corbett. Drive back to hotel. After safari fresh n up at wildlife resort, followed by hot mouth watering breakfast, after breakfast we will take you toGarjiya Mata Temple located at beautiful surrounding of Corbett National park.
