Day 01: Jaipur - Jaisalmer (560 Kms)

We will drive to Jaisalmer early in the morning. On arrival at Jaisalmer, check-in at the hotel. After fresh-n-up, we will go out for an excursion to Sand Dunes to enjoy camel safari during sunset in the vast sandy desert. In the evening, we will return back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 02: Jaisalmer

After breakfast, we will go for a sightseeing tour of Jaisalmer. We will visit the magnificent Jaisalmer fort, known as Golden fort. It is one of the only living forts in India. Further, we will see the palaces, havelis and temples inside the fort. You can have great views of Jaisalmer town from the terrace of the fort.

Day 03: Jaisalmer - Jaipur (560 Kms) - In the morning after breakfast, we will drive back to Jaipur. On arrival at Jaipur, we will drop you at your place. End of the services.
