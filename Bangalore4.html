(05 Nights / 06 Days) 1N Mysore - 2N Ooty - 2N Kodaikanal

Day 01 : Pickup from arrival and proceed to Mysore. En route visit Srirangapatna(Dariya Daulat Bagh, Gumbaz, Tippu Fort and Temple). On arrival check in to hotel. Evening proceed to Maharaja Palace & Brindavan Garden. Overnight Stay at Mysore.

Day 0 2 :After Breakfast proceed to Chamundi Hill, Big Bull & Mysore Zoo. Later proceed to Ooty. On arrival check in to hotel. Evening proceed to visit Ooty lake and Botanical Garden. Overnight Stay at Ooty.

Day 03 : After Breakfast Proceed to Coonoor. Coonoor Sight Seeing(Sim’s Park, Lam’s Rock, Dolphin Nose, Tea factory and Tea Gardens) and Doddabetta Peak. Overnight Stay at Ooty.

Day 04 : After Breakfast checkout and proceed to Kodaikanal. On arrival Check in to hotel. Evening visit to Kodaikanal Lake. Overnight Stay at Kodaikanal..

Day 05 : After Breakfast proceed to sight seeing to visit Cookers Walk, Green Valley View, Pillar’s Rocks, Silent Valley View, Silver Cascade Falls. Overnight Stay at Kodaikanal.
